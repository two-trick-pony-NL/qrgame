How to play the QR code game!

We spread more than 100 QR codes with questions over the campgrounds this bunq weekend. Each QR code contains a question for you to answer. Correct answers earns you points on the leaderboard.

Simply find and scan a QR code if you find one, if you manage to answer the question you will earn +1 point. Once answered you can answer it again, but it will not give more points. You are allowed to collaborate, as long as you're not working together with mr google (or ChatGPT for that matter). Whoever ranks #1 by sunday morning wins!

Different type of questions: Trivia or Riddle?

There are several types of questions. There are riddles and Trivia questions. Trivias require facts, Riddles require more creative answers. There is a label that tells you what is expected.
